Success Stories

Early Warning Success

Predicted the 2020-2021 drought 3 months in advance, enabling government agencies to pre-position relief supplies and save 30,000 livestock across the basin region.

Agricultural Adaptation

Farmers in Embu County shifted to drought-resistant crops based on seasonal forecasts, maintaining 80% of normal harvests during severe drought conditions.

Water Resource Management

Optimized reservoir operations during drought periods, extending water supply duration by 4 months and serving 2+ million people in Nairobi metropolitan area.
