Success Stories

Community Water Security

Mukogodo Community conservancy improved water access for 5,000+ residents through integrated watershed management combining traditional water harvesting with modern storage solutions.

Ecosystem Restoration

Restored 200 hectares of degraded rangeland using indigenous tree species, resulting in 35% increase in biodiversity and improved livestock productivity for pastoralist communities.

Energy-Water Integration

Implemented solar-powered water pumping systems serving 12 community groups, reducing energy costs by 60% while ensuring reliable water supply during dry seasons.
