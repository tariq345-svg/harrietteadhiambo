About Me

Harriette Okal

Hydrology and Water Resource Expert

Committed to advancing the vision of a resilient Africa, I am Dr. Harriette Okal, a dynamic water professional with over 8 years of experience in sustainable water resource management. My work spans diverse areas, including hydrological modeling, climate change action, data and information systems, water and sanitation, biodiversity conservation, and the Water-Energy-Food (WEF) Nexus.

Education & Credentials

  • Ph.D. in Hydrology - Rhodes University (Current)
  • M.Sc. in Land and Water Management - University of Nairobi
  • B.Sc. in Water Resource Management - University of Nairobi

Professional Impact

Throughout my career, I have fostered partnerships and raised awareness of critical issues, from remote sensing and GIS applications to water policy and environmental impact assessments. My work is rooted in empowering communities and building local capacities for managing water resources sustainably and inclusively.

Mission & Vision

I am passionate about ensuring a sustainable future for Africa's water systems and ecosystems. By integrating science, policy, and innovation, I aim to address pressing environmental challenges while fostering collaboration among professionals dedicated to impactful solutions.
