Water Resources Management & River Basin Governance

Kenya National Assessment Study

2020 - 2023 World Bank Group - $1.8M Completed

Project Impact

47 Counties Assessed
6 River Basins Analyzed
12 Government Agencies

Key Innovations

  • Governance Framework: First comprehensive river basin governance assessment for Kenya
  • Multi-Level Analysis: Integration of national, regional, and local governance structures
  • Stakeholder Mapping: Detailed analysis of 200+ water sector institutions
  • Policy Gaps Identification: Evidence-based recommendations for legislative reform

Research Achievements

4 Comprehensive policy briefs published
8 National workshops conducted
15 Policy recommendations formulated
300+ Stakeholders engaged nationally

Success Stories

Legal Framework Strengthening

Contributed to the development of the Water Act 2016 implementation guidelines, strengthening river basin management institutions and improving coordination mechanisms across all six river basins.

Inter-County Cooperation

Facilitated the establishment of 3 inter-county water resource management agreements, reducing conflicts and improving collaborative management of shared water resources.

Performance Monitoring

Developed standardized river basin performance indicators adopted by all Regional Development Authorities, enabling evidence-based decision making and adaptive management.
